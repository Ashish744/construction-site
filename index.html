Who we are

Contractors who treat your site like our own.

Constructo is an independent general contractor built around one idea: construction runs on trust as much as it runs on concrete. Our in-house crews, engineers and project managers plan every phase before a single stake goes in the ground.

  • Licensed, bonded and insured across every trade we self-perform
  • Dedicated project manager assigned from bid to handover
  • Weekly site reporting with photo and budget updates
