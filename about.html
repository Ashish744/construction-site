Our story

Started on one job site, still run the same way.

Constructo began with a single crew and a single client who wanted a contractor that answered the phone. Two decades later, we've grown to over 120 tradespeople and project staff, but the standard hasn't changed: clear estimates, honest schedules and a site superintendent who's always reachable.

We self-perform our core trades rather than subcontracting everything out, which means the people pouring your foundation and framing your structure are Constructo employees — not a rotating cast of strangers.
